# 🌍 World Generators

## Overview
Oraxen doesn't natively support ore generation. Instead, you can use world generators to spawn custom ores and blocks in your world.

## Compatibility
#### Iris World Generator
Iris World Generator features **first-class Oraxen integration**. Oraxen automatically registers as an external data provider with Iris, allowing you to reference custom blocks using the `oraxen:block_id` syntax directly in your Iris configurations.

#### Custom Ore Generator
Custom Ore Generator is a free alternative for adding custom ores to your world.

#### Terralith
Terralith is a popular datapack that overhauls world generation with new biomes and terrain features. You can integrate your Oraxen custom blocks into Terralith’s world generation by modifying the datapack files to include your custom ores and blocks.

<Callout type="important">
  For discontinued world generators (**EpicWorldGenerator**, **RealisticWorldGenerator**), see the [Archives](/compatibility/archives) section. Discontinued means that the compatibility is no longer actively maintained or removed / no longer functional.
</Callout>

## Usage
<details>
<summary><strong>Find Oraxens custom block data</strong></summary>
For most custom world generators you need to find your custom block data. This can be achieved using any minecraft version on a server running Oraxen. Just place your block (you can get it from the oraxen inventory of give it to yourself through a command) and press F3 while looking at the block you want to add.
